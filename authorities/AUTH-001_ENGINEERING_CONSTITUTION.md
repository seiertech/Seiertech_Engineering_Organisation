# AUTH-001 – Engineering Constitution

**Authority ID:** AUTH-001  
**Title:** Engineering Constitution  
**Version:** 0.1.0  
**Classification:** Constitutional Authority  
**Status:** Draft  
**Owner:** Executive Director  
**Approval Authority:** Technical Design Authority

---

## 1. Purpose

This Constitution establishes the supreme engineering authority of the SeierTech Engineering Organisation.

It defines the immutable engineering principles, governance model and constitutional requirements that govern every engineering activity undertaken by the organisation.

No engineering artefact, engineering role, engineering authority, engineering standard, engineering workflow, engineering platform, AI system or software product may conflict with this Constitution.

Where conflict exists, this Constitution shall prevail.

---

## 2. Scope

This Constitution applies to:

- Every engineering role.
- Every engineering authority.
- Every Mission Order.
- Every engineering decision.
- Every engineering artefact.
- Every software platform.
- Every AI engineering persona.
- Every engineering review.
- Every engineering build.
- Every engineering workflow.
- Every engineering knowledge source.

---

## 3. Constitutional Principles

### ENG-CON-001 — Evidence-Based Engineering

**Requirement**  
The SeierTech Engineering Organisation shall operate as an evidence-based engineering organisation.

**Rationale**  
Engineering decisions shall be based upon verifiable evidence rather than assumption, opinion or convenience.

**Verification**  
Review engineering decision records.

---

### ENG-CON-002 — Mission Traceability

**Requirement**  
Every engineering decision shall be traceable to its originating Mission Order.

**Rationale**  
Engineering decisions must remain reproducible and auditable throughout their lifecycle.

**Verification**  
Mission-to-decision traceability audit.

---

### ENG-CON-003 — Version-Controlled Artefacts

**Requirement**  
Every engineering artefact shall be maintained under version control.

**Rationale**  
Engineering knowledge shall evolve through controlled change.

**Verification**  
Repository audit.

---

### ENG-CON-004 — Architecture Before Implementation

**Requirement**  
Architecture shall precede implementation.

**Rationale**  
Engineering implementation shall not become the source of architectural truth.

**Verification**  
Architecture review.

---

### ENG-CON-005 — Knowledge as an Engineering Asset

**Requirement**  
Engineering knowledge shall be treated as a strategic engineering asset.

**Rationale**  
Knowledge is an organisational capability requiring governance, protection and continuous improvement.

**Verification**  
Knowledge governance audit.

---

### ENG-CON-006 — Mission-Originated Work

**Requirement**  
Every engineering activity shall originate from an approved Mission Order.

**Rationale**  
Engineering work shall always have a defined objective, authority and expected outcome.

**Verification**  
Mission audit.

---

### ENG-CON-007 — Constitutional Authority

**Requirement**  
Engineering authorities shall operate according to defined constitutional authority.

**Rationale**  
Authority shall derive from governance rather than individuals.

**Verification**  
Authority review.

---

### ENG-CON-008 — Independent Reviewability

**Requirement**  
Engineering decisions shall be independently reviewable.

**Rationale**  
Engineering integrity requires transparency and peer review.

**Verification**  
Decision review audit.

---

### ENG-CON-009 — Technology Independence

**Requirement**  
Engineering standards shall be technology independent wherever reasonably practicable.

**Rationale**  
The Engineering Organisation shall outlive individual technologies, frameworks and AI providers.

**Verification**  
Standards review.

---

### ENG-CON-010 — Integrity Over Speed

**Requirement**  
Engineering integrity shall take precedence over delivery speed.

**Rationale**  
Long-term engineering quality is of greater organisational value than short-term delivery convenience.

**Verification**  
Engineering governance review.

---

## 4. Constitutional Authority Hierarchy

The Engineering Constitution is the highest authority within the SeierTech Engineering Organisation.

All future authority documents derive their authority from this Constitution.

No subordinate authority may conflict with this document.

---

## 5. Change Control

This Constitution shall only be amended through formal Engineering Change Authority approval.

Every constitutional amendment shall:

- possess a unique identifier;
- include rationale;
- include impact assessment;
- include approval record;
- maintain complete revision history.

---

## 6. Status

This is Version 0.1.0 of the Engineering Constitution.

It is the first constitutional draft and shall be expanded through controlled authority development.
