# DEC-000001 – EMS Repository Pivot

**Decision ID:** DEC-000001  
**Status:** Accepted  
**Mission:** MISSION-0001  
**Date:** 2026-06-29

## Decision

The SeierTech Engineering Organisation repository shall be treated as an Engineering Management System rather than a simple document repository.

## Rationale

A document-first repository would not provide sufficient traceability, organisational memory, indexed decision history or long-term engineering governance.

The EMS model establishes authorities, registers, libraries, operations, workforce, platforms, standards and work products as first-class engineering capabilities.

## Impact

Future repository work shall be organised around EMS capabilities, not convenience folders.

## Outcome

Accepted and recorded in the Decision Register.
