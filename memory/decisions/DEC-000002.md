# DEC-000002 – Platform Intake and Mission Spine

**Decision ID:** DEC-000002  
**Status:** Accepted  
**Mission:** MISSION-001  
**Date:** 2026-06-29

## Decision

Before any mission can be issued against a platform, the platform must complete Platform Intake.

Platform Intake is itself a mission. It reads the platform repository, derives a platform record, runs persona extraction, creates the Mission Spine, raises founder questions where evidence is incomplete, and updates the Readiness Register.

## Rationale

The EMS requires reliable platform context before specialist personas can operate safely and consistently.

The Mission Spine prevents future missions from starting with incomplete or inconsistent platform knowledge.

## Loop Engineering

This decision supports Loop Engineering: doctrine governs personas, personas execute missions, missions produce builds, builds are verified, and results feed back into the EMS.

## Impact

Platforms remain locked until intake readiness is complete.
