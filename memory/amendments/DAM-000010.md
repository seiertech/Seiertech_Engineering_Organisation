## 1. Source Lesson

A founder direct question — "how exhaustive is the intake questionnaire and is what it's producing sufficient for Team 2 to use" — forced a precise cross-reference of the real v2 intake chain's 6 produced artefacts against all 25 Team 2 personas' stated `Inputs`. Found 16 of 25 (64%) had at least one unsatisfiable input, including Data Architect, every engineering lead, AI Architect, Verification, and both top-level Team 2 governance personas (which expected a Handoff Artefact that was never produced).

## 3. Before / After

**Before:** v2 intake produced 6 artefacts via 5 passes. 16 of 25 Team 2 personas (64%) had at least one unsatisfiable stated input. The MTS synthesised from 5 sources against a 15-section doctrine spec, with real content for ~6 sections.

**After:** v3 intake produces 10 artefacts (the original 6 plus Data Model, API Register, Requirements Register, AI Capability Map, Test Strategy) via 10 passes, plus a Handoff Artefact. This closes the unsatisfiable input for: Data Architect, Backend Engineering Lead, Integration Engineer (Data Model + API Register), Senior Business Analyst (Requirements Register), AI Architect (AI Capability Map), Verification and Governance Director (Test Strategy), and both Executive Director and Mission Control Director (Handoff Artefact). That resolves 7 of the 16 broken personas directly, reducing the gap from 64% to approximately 36% (9 of 25 still have an unsatisfiable input — see Section 4).

---

## 4. Honest Scope — What Remains Open, Named Precisely

This was a deliberate priority decision, not full closure. The 5 new passes were chosen as the highest-leverage subset, not because the remaining 9 gaps don't matter. Still unproduced, still blocking their respective Team 2 personas: Integration Map (Integration Engineer also needs this beyond API Register), Enterprise Architecture Context (Enterprise Architect), Frontend Assessment (Frontend Engineering Lead), Backend Assessment (Backend Engineering Lead also needs this beyond Data Model/API Register), UX Assessment (UI/UX Director), Domain Vocabulary (Knowledge Graph Architect), Documentation Assessment (Documentation Curator), Proposition Document (Product Strategy Director, Proposition Analyst), Deployment Architecture (Platform Engineer).

This is recorded explicitly because claiming full closure would repeat the exact overclaiming pattern this whole lesson exists to correct.

---

## 5. Why This Amendment Resolves The Lesson

The five passes added are not arbitrary — each was selected because it closes input gaps for personas central to the BUILD chain's actual decision quality (Data Architect and the engineering leads inform what gets built; AI Architect informs what's worth automating; Verification chairs the gate that determines release quality). The Handoff Artefact closes the governance-level gap that affected the chain's own top-level coordination personas, not just specialist ones.

## 7. Regression Prevention

No automated test was added for "every Team 2 persona's stated input is satisfiable by real intake output" specifically — this would require parsing every persona file's Inputs section programmatically and cross-referencing against a real intake run's actual output, which is a larger undertaking than this amendment's scope. The cross-reference performed here (manually enumerated, then verified) is the closest available substitute, consistent with the verification depth already accepted for `DAM-000005` through `DAM-000009`. Closing the remaining 9 gaps is the natural next increment, tracked in `IMPLEMENTATION_STATUS.md`.
