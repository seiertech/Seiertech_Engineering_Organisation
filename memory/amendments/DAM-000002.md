## 1. Source Lesson

A doctrine inventory audit (compiling a single index of every standard/authority/persona/operation/register in the repo) found 26 legacy/draft artefacts alongside their properly-built replacements. Two — Risk Register and Decision Register — were not actually dead: they were referenced as live, load-bearing artefacts across multiple authorities and personas despite never having been formally built as `REG-NNNNNN` artefacts. Separately, a vocabulary audit running STD-000004's own prohibited-terms list against the full doctrine corpus found "QA" used 21 times as the literal formal title of a persona (PER-000016) — a direct, unambiguous violation of the standard that defines "QA" as prohibited.

## 3. Before / After

**Before:** Multiple authorities and personas said "tracked in the Risk Register" / "Platform Decision Register" with no such artefact actually existing as a conformant `REG-NNNNNN` register — only an early, unreferenced draft sat in `registers/RISK_REGISTER.md` and `registers/DECISION_REGISTER.md`.

**After:** `REG-000008` and `REG-000009` exist, fully conformant, and every informal reference across 8 files now cites them by proper artefact ID.

**Before:** PER-000016's title was "QA and Governance Director" — directly contradicting STD-000004 Section 4, which lists "QA" as prohibited (use: Verification) in the very same document.

**After:** Renamed to "Verification and Governance Director" (Team 1) / "Verification & Governance Director" (Team 2) across both folder names and all cross-references.

---

## 4. Why This Amendment Resolves The Lesson

The register gap is resolved at the structural level, not just documented — the artefacts now exist and are correctly referenced, so future personas reading "REG-000008" will find a real register rather than a dead end. The vocabulary violation is resolved at its source (the persona's actual title) rather than patched at individual mention sites, which is what prevents it from silently reappearing the next time someone writes a new cross-reference to this persona.

## 6. Regression Prevention

No automated test was added for this specific amendment (it is a one-time naming/structural fix, not an ongoing behavioural risk). The general prevention mechanism is `OPR-000012` itself — see DAM-000003 — plus the recommendation that any future conformance audit script (not yet built) include a prohibited-vocabulary scan and a register-existence-vs-reference cross-check as standing checks.
