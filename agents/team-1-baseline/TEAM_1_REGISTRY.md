## Purpose

Team 1 exists for one purpose — establish the baseline. They activate on MISSION-001, go deep into the platform reality, extract everything, create what doesn't exist, reconcile all governance, and deliver the definitive baseline picture. When the handoff artefact is signed off, Team 1 stands down. Team 2 takes over permanently.

Team 1 never fires on forward missions. They are the intake strike force — forensic, thorough, and temporary.
