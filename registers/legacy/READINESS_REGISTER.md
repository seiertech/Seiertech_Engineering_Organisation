# Readiness Register

**Register ID:** REG-READY  
**Status:** Draft

## Purpose

This register tracks whether platforms are ready to receive EMS missions.

A platform is locked until Platform Intake is complete, the Mission Spine is complete, and founder questions are resolved.

## Readiness States

- Intake Required

- Questions Pending
- Spine Incomplete
- Ready
- Locked

## Rules

No mission may be issued against a platform unless Mission Access is marked Unlocked.
