### 2.1 Human Completion Instructions

Use this template to record formal Verification of an Engineering Delivery Package against its Mission, Standards, acceptance criteria, and Quality Gates. Evidence must be recorded for every gate checked.

### 2.2 Agent Persona Completion Instructions

Complete from the Engineering Delivery Package, Mission, applied Standards, acceptance criteria, and available evidence. Do not mark PASS unless every required gate has supporting evidence. Use STD-000004 vocabulary only.
